Red Kampot Pepper offers a sweeter and milder taste compared to the black variety, boasting a well-rounded flavor profile. Its fruity aromas, coupled with sweet notes of red fruit and honey, make it the perfect companion for enhancing the flavors of salads, chocolate, and fruit desserts.

Black Kampot Pepper, a robust character with delicate nuances, delights the palate with its profound and lingering flavours. Discover hints of eucalyptus and fresh mint that dance on your tongue, leaving a lasting sensation. This exquisite spice is the ideal companion for grilled meats, working its magic when freshly ground onto the plate.

White Kampot Pepper, crafted by delicately soaking the red pepper for 4 to 5 days to remove its outer coat. It holds subtle notes of fresh herbs and zesty lemon. This enchanting spice pairs beautifully with any seafood.
